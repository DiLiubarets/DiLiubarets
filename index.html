Recently most of my day is spent experimenting with JavaScript (and its endless list of frameworks). I love coding and the challenge of learning something new every day. If I'm not coding, I'm filming, taking pictures and traveling most of my daily life I post in my instagram blog. I have been practicing law in Ukraine for 3 years.

I often use a quotation, "Be the CEO of your life" (Robin Sharma), and I think people should take this on board. Follow yours passion.
