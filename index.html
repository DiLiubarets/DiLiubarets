Hello, I am Dina, and I love to do everything. Most of my day is spent experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (and its endless list of frameworks). I love coding and the challenge of learning something new every day. If I'm not coding, I'm filming and taking pictures. If I'm not doing that too, I'm traveling or practicing law or reading stars.

I am the person who wants to make the most out of life and help others do that too. Most of my life was spent realizing that it is not essential to stick to one particular field. One can multitask and give time to all of the fields they like. We do not realize that our hobbies are actually our passions, and we should pursue them and not some orthodox profession that the professor told us.

If we can not experiment with our own life, we can never expect to change anything in life, neither ours nor someone else's. I usually say a quotation: "Be the CEO of your life." (Robin Sharma). And I think people should read this. I would conclude with the fact that I have done a lot in my life and went after almost every passion I had, and today I can say that I did something in my life. I want all of you to feel that and do whatever you want. So just go and do whatever you want in life, and make sure that you are always following your passion and not any materialistic object.
