about me

My name is Dina Liubarets, and I am a multi-professional businesswoman as well as an entrepreneur.

I am a lawyer, as well as a coder. I was in Ukraine while studying law and finished Law school over there. Since childhood, I had a strong attraction towards law and computers, but when I grew up, things never turned out to be the way I wanted them to be. It seemed as if I had to choose between Law and Computer. So initially, I decided to take law as a Computer profession that can be pursued at any age. After getting over with my Law School in Ukraine, I practiced law for 3 years. And of course, I had to, what would be the point of studying law if you were not to practice it. Anyway, I have to admit that my experience while practicing was terrific. I saw a series of mixed events, some of the cases I won and some I lost, but they gave me a lot of experience and helped to polish and cook for the working world. A significant milestone in my life was in 2013, when I decided to move to Canada. It had already been six years since I was managing and growing my family business. I had loads of responsibility on my shoulders, but my passion for computers sparked again. And this time it was something a lot more specific, I developed an interest in coding websites. I learned to code, and suddenly this was a passion that I could not get rid of.

In 2020 I decided to join a 24-week coding boot camp at Carleton University. That experience was so overwhelming for me. I loved to sit in front of a computer and code and solve all those problems. My life had an empty spot for technology, and coding filled that, and I suddenly felt complete. Tech is now an integral part of my life, and I love to learn new things and experiment. Moreover, it is also very satisfying and comfortable when I'm learning or building new stuff or programs. It has been some years since I decided that the world should know about me and the achievements I've made in life.

So, I made an Instagram blog where I share posts and videos regarding my work and life and allow other people to get motivated so they can do something similar. I also have a passion for exploring the world. Hence, my love for traveling and the need to post on social media get along very well. I appreciate and appreciate the photos, videos, adventures, and fun that come with my travel. If said honestly, I would love to keep this up for the rest of my life. We can not deny the fact that fashion is one of the elementary parts of social media. What a productive coincidence that fashion is also an essential part of my life, and this sense of fashion allows me to post quality pictures and videos and set new trends for my audience to follow.

After all that I did in my life and the victories I've won, I can confidently and proudly say that I have good leadership and management skills. I can quickly analyze situations, deduce possible outcomes and consequences of the decision, and take suitable and appropriate actions. All the people who worked with me have learned a lot from me, and the same goes for me. I am very responsible in every field of life. Even though all this responsibility is sometimes too much to handle, I am very open about myself. This behavior also allows me to have an interactive and productive relationship with my coworkers and family members. Another trait that I like is that I can manage time efficiently. I have to admit that the responsibility I took from such a young age taught me many things, one of which was the value of time. I am ready to welcome new things in my life, but I also do not forget to maintain the old ones' importance. Along with fashion, photography, law, and coding, I also know how to read stars.
